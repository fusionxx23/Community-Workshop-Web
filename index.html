Summary

Community Workshop is a space designed to support the collaborative, entrepreneurial and artistic needs of makers, hobbyists, entrepreneurs, artists, and small business owners. We provide the tools, materials, space, and collaboration required to turn your idea into a finished project.

A makerspace by definition, is many things, and they each have their focus. Our focus leans toward technology and how it can enhance the arts, society, and our community. We balance that out with sailing, music, and laughter.

We are all about shared resources. All of the tools and facilities are available for every member. If you are unfamiliar with a tool or process we will make apprenticeships and training available

About

Community Workshop is an inclusive space bringing people together to share resources and support each other in the exploration of shared interests and development of new skills.

Tools

Our main focus is on technology, but our interests are wide and varied. We are building out our inventory based on what our project needs are. We have several 3D printers, a workable wood shop, and steel fabrication tools. We are currently building a laser cutter, benchtop mill, and a CNC plasma cutter.
